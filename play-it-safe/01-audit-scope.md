## Audit Framework
This internal audit is based on the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (NIST CSF).

## Scope
The scope of this audit includes the entire security program at Botium Toys, including:

- On-premises IT equipment
- Employee end-user devices
- Internal network and internet access
- Data retention and storage systems
- Ecommerce and inventory management systems
- Legacy systems requiring manual monitoring

## Audit Goals
- Identify assets managed by the IT department
- Evaluate existing security controls
- Assess compliance with relevant regulations
- Identify risks that may impact business continuity
